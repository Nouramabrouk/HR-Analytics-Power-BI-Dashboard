# HR Analytics Dashboard - Employee Retention & Performance

# Project Overview
This Power BI dashboard provides a 360-degree view of Human Resources data to help organizations understand employee attrition, satisfaction, and performance trends. The project translates 16 key business requirements (KPIs) into actionable visual insights.

# Key Features & KPIs Implemented
The dashboard is divided into three main analytical areas:

# 1. Overview & Retention
Attrition Rate Analysis: Monitoring the percentage of employees leaving the company.

Distance from Home vs. Attrition: Using a Scatter Plot to analyze the impact of commute distance on turnover.

Gender Distribution: A Donut Chart showing the workforce gender balance.

Business Travel Impact: Identifying frequent travelers and their distribution across departments.

# 2. Satisfaction & Work-Life Balance
Departmental Job Satisfaction: A Heatmap comparing satisfaction scores across different departments.

Work-Life Balance Rating: Analyzing balance scores by Job Role to identify high-stress positions.

Overtime Impact: Evaluating how overtime affects overall employee satisfaction.

Job Involvement: Distribution of involvement scores using a Pie Chart.

# 3. Performance & Compensation
Compensation vs. Performance: Analyzing the correlation between monthly income and performance ratings.

Income Growth: A Waterfall Chart showing how education level impacts average monthly income.

Top Talent & Improvement Lists: Dedicated tables identifying High Performers (Rating 4) and those needing development.

Age Distribution: Understanding the workforce demographics through age group binning.

# Tools Used
Power BI Desktop: Data modeling, DAX measures, and visualization.

Power Query: Data cleaning and transformation.

DAX: Created custom measures for Attrition Rate, Average Satisfaction, and Performance Metrics.
